PRECISION TACTICAL BLACK JOGGER PANTS

Introducing the Precision Tactical Black Jogger Pants, available in medium, large, and extra large sizes. These pants are crafted from durable materials, ensuring both comfort and a perfect fit through the cotton and cargo blend fabric. Boasting a sleek matte black color, they are ideal for a stylish streetwear or techwear aesthetic. The pants feature a convenient belt and buckle closure, while the left leg showcases an embroidered I-tech logo, complemented by a precision mark design. Additionally, drop down straps on both legs allow for adjustable thigh tightness, while elastic ankle cuffs provide a secure fit.
