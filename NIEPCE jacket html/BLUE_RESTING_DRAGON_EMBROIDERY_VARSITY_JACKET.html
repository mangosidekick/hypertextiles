BLUE RESTING DRAGON EMBROIDERY VARSITY JACKET


The BLUE RESTING DRAGON EMBROIDERY VARSITY JACKET is available in sizes ranging from s to 3xl and features a convenient zipper closure. It offers a regular true-to-size fit, resembling a classic baseball jacket. Inspired by East Asian culture, the jacket showcases embroidery designs of resting dragons on the back. It is constructed with a middle layer made of regular cotton blend and an outer layer crafted from heavy-duty cargo material. Additionally, the front chest displays an I-Tech embroidered logo design.

In summary, the BLUE RESTING DRAGON EMBROIDERY VARSITY JACKET is a stylish option for individuals seeking a unique garment. Its sizing options and zipper closure ensure a comfortable fit. Drawing inspiration from East Asian culture, the jacket boasts elaborate embroidery of resting dragons on its back. The construction consists of a cotton blend middle layer and a sturdy cargo material outer layer. Furthermore, an additional touch of branding is added through the I-Tech embroidered logo design on the front chest.
