UC PATCHWORK FUNCTIONAL HOODIE

The UC Patchwork Functional Hoodie is offered in a variety of sizes ranging from small to 3XL. It is skillfully stitched and showcases patchwork designs created using top-of-the-line EX sewing machines. The design of the hoodie includes three generously-sized pockets on the front, providing additional storage space while adding to its fashionable streetwear and techwear aesthetic. Crafted from 100% Supersoft ringspun cotton, this hoodie boasts high-quality fabric that is durable enough to withstand years of wear and tear. Moreover, it features double full zippers that extend down the front of the jacket, adding both style and functionality.

100% Supersoft ringspun cotton is the material of choice for the UC Patchwork Functional Hoodie, ensuring a comfortable and luxurious feel against the skin. The hoodie exemplifies superior craftsmanship through its impeccable stitching and intricate patchwork designs achieved using EX sewing machines. Its design is not only visually appealing but also functional, with the inclusion of three generously-sized pockets on the front that offer ample storage space. Suitable for those seeking a trendy streetwear or techwear appearance, this hoodie is constructed to endure and has the potential to become a lifelong wardrobe staple. With double full zippers running down the front, this hoodie features a practical and stylish element that further enhances its overall appeal.
