NEV Tilt Big Pocket Cargo Pants

Introducing the NEV Tilt Big Pocket Cargo Pants, available in two elegant shades, grey and black. These pants are designed to provide both style and functionality, making them a versatile addition to any wardrobe. With waist sizes ranging from 28 to 32, you can find the perfect fit for your comfort. Crafted from a blend of high-quality materials, including 62% cotton, 35% nylon, and 3% spandex, these pants offer durability and stretchability.

The NEV Tilt Big Pocket Cargo Pants feature a unique design element with their big pockets, adding a touch of fashion-forwardness to your look. Whether you're heading out for a casual day out or need a reliable pair of pants for outdoor activities, these cargo pants are the perfect choice. Embrace comfort, style, and practicality all in one with the NEV Tilt Big Pocket Cargo Pants.
